## Implications

- **HCI:** content credential / watermark UI design、creator-side disclosure affordance — CHI 跟 trust calibration 研究
- **CS:** watermark + provenance benchmark 缺廣告 scenario — IR / security / NLP 都會接
- **Critical Data Studies:** 誰決定什麼算 'synthetic'？合成性的定義權是 epistemic governance 核心

## Key findings

- EU AI Act 把 commercial deepfake 分到 'limited-risk' + disclosure / provenance 義務、2026-08 生效
- 46 個美國州 通過 deepfake 立法；federal TAKE IT DOWN Act 2025-05 簽署
- NY synthetic-performer disclosure law 2025-12-11 簽
- FTC：AI persona endorsement 適用 Endorsement Guides、不揭露 = 欺騙性
- C2PA content credentials + Aura DPP 兩條路收斂到 'provenance metadata 作 governance infrastructure'
- TikTok 把 'trustworthy' 從平台目標移除、擴大 AI endorsement creator policy (2025)
- 中國 Deep Synthesis Provisions 強制 watermarking — EU / US / China 三 regime split 可 map
